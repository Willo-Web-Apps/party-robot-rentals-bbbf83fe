# Design DNA — RoboFête

## Visual Personality: bold-energetic
## Layout: product-led
## Typography: bold-display

### Color Palette
- Primary: #FF3D6B (electric rose/hot pink) → oklch(0.60 0.26 10)
- Accent: #FFD23F (electric gold/amber) → oklch(0.87 0.20 88)
- Surface: #0D0D1A (deep midnight) → oklch(0.09 0.02 280)
- Text: #F0F0FF (near-white with cool cast) → oklch(0.95 0.01 280)

### Font Pairing
- Display: Archivo Black — weight 900 (hero headlines, punch, impact)
- Body: Barlow — weights 400/500/600 (supporting text, readable at speed)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Neon glow halos on brand elements**: electric-rose glow (box-shadow with spread) on CTAs and badges — differentiates from flat/corporate competitors and signals energy
2. **Asymmetric hero layout**: headline at massive scale (text-8xl) bleeds with glowing decorative orbs offset behind text — breaks the centered-everything pattern
3. **Robot package cards with neon-top border accent**: each card has a vivid top border in a unique color (pink, gold, cyan) against dark midnight surface — creates hierarchy without a rainbow
4. **Scrolling ticker social proof bar**: horizontal scrolling banner of event types served — dynamic energy that flat stat blocks can't match
5. **Section dividers as light beams**: thin gradient horizontal rules that fade from the primary color to transparent — reinforces the "electric light" theme
